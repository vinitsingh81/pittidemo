PRECISION CRAFTED FOR PEAK PERFORMANCE

Leading the Way in Electrical Steel Laminations and Comprehensive Solutions Since 1983

With a legacy spanning over four decades, we are a trusted leader in the manufacturing of precision-engineered components for rotating electrical equipment. Established in 1983, we began our journey specializing in electrical steel laminations for motors used across a diverse range of electrical applications. Today, we have evolved into a comprehensive solutions provider, offering a wide portfolio.

Our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as the market leader in the lamination segment—a critical component in the performance and efficiency of electric motors and generators. Driven by engineering excellence and a deep understanding of industry requirements, we continue to power progress in the electrical manufacturing sector, supporting a wide spectrum of industries worldwide.

Built to Perform, Engineerd to Last - From Metal to Masterpiece, We Build Excellence and Deliver Innovation. Your One-stop Solution for Every Precision Part.

Committed to integrity, innovation, and accountability, we prioritize teamwork and customer satisfaction. We continuously improve, embrace transparency, and foster a culture of excellence to deliver seamless, reliable, and valuable solutions for diverse industries.

Applications

Our diverse product portfolio supports a wide range of industrial applications, enabling us to serve evolving market needs and deliver value across multiple sectors.
We cater to a broad spectrum of industries including: Appliances, Cement, Construction, Data Centres, DG Sets, Electric Vehicle Motors, Freight Rail, Hydro Generators, Lift Irrigation, Medical Equipment, Mining, Mass Urban Transport, Oxygen Plants, Passenger Rail, Pumps, Steel, Sugar, Thermal Power, Windmill Generators, and Wind Energy..

Windmills, or wind turbines, provide clean, renewable energy by harnessing wind power. They drastically cut greenhouse gas emissions compared to fossil fuels, reducing air pollution and combating climate change. Additionally, they use minimal land and water, preserving ecosystems without generating waste.
Mining machines, like robust excavators and backhoe loaders, excel in harsh underground environments. They dig, load ore, and handle materials efficiently with powerful hydraulics and diesel engines. These versatile rigs boost productivity while minimizing downtime in coal and metal extraction.
Diesel generator (DG) sets deliver reliable backup power during outages, powering industries, hospitals, and homes. Available in 5-2000 kVA ratings, they run on diesel engines coupled with alternators for efficient electricity generation. Modern units feature low-emission tech for better sustainability.
Thermal power plants efficiently generate reliable electricity using abundant fuels like coal and gas. They provide stable baseload power, support economic growth, and operate flexibly near resources, ensuring continuous energy supply for industries and homes.
Electric vehicles (EVs) revolutionize transportation with zero tailpipe emissions, reducing air pollution and fossil fuel dependence. Powered by advanced lithium-ion batteries, they offer longer ranges, rapid charging, and lower operating costs. EVs promote sustainability, cut greenhouse gases, and integrate smart tech for efficient driving.
Data centers are secure facilities housing servers for cloud computing, AI, and data storage. They enable global connectivity, process massive data volumes, and drive digital innovation. Modern designs prioritize energy efficiency with cooling tech and renewables to minimize environmental impact
Mass metro systems efficiently transport millions daily, slashing traffic congestion and emissions. They cut air pollution, save fuel, boost economic growth, and enhance urban connectivity. Affordable, safe, and reliable, metros foster sustainable cities and improve commuter lives.
Medical equipment like MRI scanners, ventilators, and infusion pumps revolutionizes healthcare. They enable precise diagnostics, life-saving treatments, and patient monitoring. Advanced devices improve accuracy, reduce recovery times, and enhance outcomes in hospitals worldwide.
